## Analysis indicators[​](#analysis-indicators "Direct link to Analysis indicators")

The [analysis indicator](/lightweight-charts/tutorials/analysis-indicators) examples below serve as a starting point for creating your own indicators.
You can use them directly in your projects.
Follow the links to see the indicators' source code on GitHub.

To run them locally, follow the [setup instructions](https://github.com/tradingview/lightweight-charts/tree/master/indicator-examples/) in the repository.
You can also explore each indicator in action on a [live demo page](https://tradingview.github.io/lightweight-charts/indicator-examples/).

[## Average Price

Calculates the average of prices (e.g., open, high, low, close)](https://github.com/tradingview/lightweight-charts/tree/master/indicator-examples/src/indicators/average-price)

[## Correlation

Measures the statistical relationship between two data series](https://github.com/tradingview/lightweight-charts/tree/master/indicator-examples/src/indicators/correlation)

[## Median Price

Returns the median (middle) value between high and low prices](https://github.com/tradingview/lightweight-charts/tree/master/indicator-examples/src/indicators/median-price)

[## Momentum

Measures the rate of change in price over time](https://github.com/tradingview/lightweight-charts/tree/master/indicator-examples/src/indicators/momentum)

[## Simple Moving Average

Smooths data by averaging values over a fixed period](https://github.com/tradingview/lightweight-charts/tree/master/indicator-examples/src/indicators/moving-average)

[## Percent Change

Shows the relative change between two values as a percentage](https://github.com/tradingview/lightweight-charts/tree/master/indicator-examples/src/indicators/percent-change)

[## Product

Multiplies a series of values](https://github.com/tradingview/lightweight-charts/tree/master/indicator-examples/src/indicators/product)

[## Ratio

Divides one value by another](https://github.com/tradingview/lightweight-charts/tree/master/indicator-examples/src/indicators/ratio)

[## Spread

Calculates the difference between two values](https://github.com/tradingview/lightweight-charts/tree/master/indicator-examples/src/indicators/spread)

[## Sum

Adds up a series of values](https://github.com/tradingview/lightweight-charts/tree/master/indicator-examples/src/indicators/sum)

[## Weighted Close

Calculates a weighted average of high, low, and close prices](https://github.com/tradingview/lightweight-charts/tree/master/indicator-examples/src/indicators/weighted-close)
